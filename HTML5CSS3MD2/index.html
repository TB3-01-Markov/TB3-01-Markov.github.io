OUR MENU

CHICKEN

Chicken can be cooked in a variety of ways, including roasting, grilling, grilling, frying, boiling, and etc. Chicken meat has come to dominate many cuisines due to the relative ease and low cost of growing it. It is the most widespread type of poultry in the world. Chicken has lower concentrations of cholesterol and saturated fat than red meat.

BEEF

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle. Beef can be prepared in various ways; cuts are often used for steak, which can be cooked to varying degrees of doneness, while trimmings are often ground or minced, as found in most hamburgers. Beef contains protein, iron, and vitamin B12. Beef is the third most widely consumed meat in the world.

SUSHI

Sushi is a Japanese traditional dish. Sushi is traditionally made with medium-grain white vinegared rice. It is very often prepared with seafood, such as squid, eel, yellowtail, salmon, tuna, or imitation crab meat. It is often served with pickled ginger, wasabi, and soy sauce. Daikon radish or pickled daikon are popular garnishes for the dish.
